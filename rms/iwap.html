Access an international database of persons who have criminal records or outstanding warrants in Loquntia and cooperative Micronations.

International Warrants, Arrests, and Prosecutions Record (IWAPS)

The Royal Marshals Service has made this online database to help the international effort of fighting crime, and to assist micronations with identifying serial offenders who commit international crimes. Search Database

For more refined results, try the following:

  • To search for a person with outstanding warrants, place an asterisk ("*") before their last name
  • Names are listed in the format [last name], [first name and middle name] (e.g. DOE, JOHN QUINTON)
  • Dates are listed in the format [DD MON YYYY] (e.g. 08 FEB 2014)
  • Pending arrests, warrants, or prosecutions will have an asterisk ("*") before the data
  • Arrests, warrants, indictments, and convictions will have an "A:", "W:", "I:", or "C:" in front of them, respectively

If you have any reason to believe information in this database to be false or incorrect, or you would like to know how to eliminate a warrant, please contact loquntia@gmail.com.
