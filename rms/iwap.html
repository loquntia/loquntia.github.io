For more refined results, try the following:

  • To search for a person with outstanding warrants, place an asterisk ("*") before their last name
  • Names are listed in the format [last name], [first name and middle name] (e.g. DOE, JOHN QUINTON)
  • Dates are listed in the format [DD MON YYYY] (e.g. 08 FEB 2014)
  • Pending arrests, warrants, or prosecutions will have an asterisk ("*") before the data
  • Arrests, warrants, indictments, and convictions will have an "A:", "W:", "I:", or "C:" in front of them, respectively
