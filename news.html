New Department of Collections, Forfeiture and Compensations (MEx CFC)

A new department of the Ministry of the Exchequer has been formed with helping citizens deal with debts, delinquent tax, and asset forfeiture in mind. If you owe more than 3 crown to a party, that party may file a claim with collections. Also, all taxes which haven’t been paid, criminal penalties that haven’t been served, & c. will be filed with the CFC.
