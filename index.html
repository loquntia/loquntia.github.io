Junio 20, 2014

Government website is undergoing a massive makeover.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs Department of International Correspondence is giving the government website a massive makeover from the ground up.

Because this site is under construction, you may have some issues accessing government resources. To access the Kingdom of Loquntia website as though it were before the change, please go to loquntia.github.io/old/. All content will be kept there as-is until the transfer is complete.

Many government records accessed by the Kingdom of Loquntia's website have been stored on a separate server. The software of the server is either deprecated, or the exploits that are used have been closed down. Because of this, many government records including case transcripts, & c. have become unavailable on our website. This has come about shortly before the decision to give the site a makeover was conceived, so as we continue the makeover process, we will also be uploading government documents online so they can be accessed on our website.

If you need access to government records and cannot access them during this makeover process, you are advised to use the link below under Services to Request Government Documents.

If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please feel free to email us at loquntia@gmail.com.
