Survey

The Ministry of the Exchequer's "Market Basket Appraisal Survey" (MBAS) is a survey which briefly asks for people to appraise what they would pay for a basket of goods. All users of this converter are encouraged to participate in this survey and give us data for as many currencies as they can. This data is then used to calculate exchange rates which are used by the calculator on this site to convert between any two of the currencies we have in our database. Exchange rates change rapidly, and inflation is volatile, and to ensure that the rates available on this site are up to date, it is requested that some take this survey on a regular basis when convenient.

To have your currency featured on this converter, it needs to have active survey participants who can provide data.
