Library of Blue Ink

Public and private archives, recordkeeping, and publications

The Library of Blue Ink was founded as a private publishing and recordkeeping agency in 2011 and was given to the Crown of Loquntia as a national archives agency. The Library of Blue Ink now currently serves as the national archives service for Loquntia, a central recordkeeping office for all Loquntian government agencies, and has since formed its educational facilities into the University of Loquntia, among other endeavours.
