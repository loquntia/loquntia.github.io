Completing the EM990 Application for Military Enlistment and Contract

  1. Taxable Person's Serial Number. First, you will be asked to supply your TPSN if you have one. This will make it easier for the reviewer of your application to identify you in inter-agency records.
  2. Family Name. Your family name, or last name is required. (e.g. "SAMPLE" in "JOHN QUINTON SAMPLE")
  3. Given Name. (e.g. "JOHN QUINTON" in "JOHN QUINTON SAMPLE")
  4. Date of Birth. Your date of birth is required. If you do not know the exact date of your birth, please try to get as close as possible to it. If you are a minor under the age of twelve (12), you will be rejected.
  5. Email Address. We will contact you shortly after submission of your application to tell you if you were accepted or rejected, and to give you your UCC code, among other important information.
  6. Military Branch. Here you will select the branch of the military you will be applying with. If your application is approved by the Ministry of Defence, but is not approved by the branch of the military you applied with it will automatically be referred to the other. (e.g. if you applied with the Army and were rejected, your application will be forwarded to the Navy.)
  7. Emergency Contact Name. Give us the name of an emergency contact we can email if we cannot get a hold of you or if there is an emergency.
  8. Emergency Contact Email Address. We will also need their email address. We will only use this contact in an emergency.
  9. Questionnaire Questions (M990A). You will be asked to answer eleven (4) questions about yourself iin this section regarding your interests and your medical history.
    • Expertise. What are your strengths and talents?
    • Height. How tall are you in feet and inches (e.g. 5 FT 10 IN)
    • Weight. How much do you weight? (e.g. 140)
    • Medical History. Please state ALL medical, psychological or developmental disorders or disabilities which you have had a history of or currently have. (e.g asthma, scoliosis, brain damage, serotonin-deficient depression, cleidocranial displasia, & c.). Your answer(s) to this question do not necessarily disqualify you from enlistment. (e.g. "I had asthma starting around the second grade, and grew out of it through middle school.")
  10. Questionnaire Questions (M990B). You will be asked to answer thirteen (13) questions about yourself in this section.
    • Have you ever been convicted of a crime in the Kingdom of Loquntia? If so, please explain. (Text Box)
    • I have a criminal record on file with the Loquntian Royal Marshals Service. (Tick the box if you have a record on file)
    • I have never been a member of or associated myself with the Communist Party.
    • I have never been a member of a terrorist organisation.
    • I have never directly or indirectly advocated the overthrow of a government by force or use of violence.
    • I have never persecuted either directly or indirectly any person because of race, religion, national origin, membership, or political opinion.
    • Between March 23, 1933 and May 8, 1945, I did not work for or associate in any way with the Nazi government of Germany or any government in any area occupied by, allied with, or established with the help of the Nazi government in Germany.
    • I have never been involved in genocide, torture, killing, or attempting to kill someone, badly hurting or attempting to hurt someone on purpose, or forcing or attempting to force someone to have sexual relations.
    • I have never impeded in the practice of someone else's religion.
    • I have never sponsored, facilitated, or carried out the abortion of a foetus.
    • I have not ended a human life outside of medical practice within the last 6,322 days.
    • I have never escaped from a jail, prison, or other place of incarceration.
    • I have never escaped from an institution for mental health.
  11. Terms and Conditions of Enlistment Contract. In order to apply for enlistment in the Royal Military you will need to sign the Terms and Conditions of Enlistment Contract. The full text of the contract is available below:

    CONTRACT FOR ENLISTMENT OR REENLISTMENT IN THE ARMED FORCES OF THE KINGDOM OF LOQUNTIA.

    PARTICIPATION IN THE ASSOCIATED LEGAL INSTRUMENTS AS HEREIN DEFINED IS PROHIBITED EXCEPT UPON EXECUTION OF, AND CONCORDANCE WITH, THIS CONTRACTUAL AGREEMENT; AND UPON PARTICIPATION YOU AGREE TO ALL TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF THIS CONTRACT AND ALL LAWS AND CUSTOMS ASSOCIATED WITH THE ROYAL MILITARY OF THE KINGDOM OF LOQUNTIA.

    CITIZENSHIP.
    Personnel who are enlisted in the Royal Military will, except as provided by military customs, be treated as though they were a Loquntian citizen. When a party has been honourably dismissed from the Royal Military, they will be granted Loquntian Citizenship (Code 10.II.4.1.F) except as provided by law.

    SUBORDINATION.
    The undersigned party agrees to defend and support the constitution of the Kingdom of Loquntia and obey any and all lawful orders, express or otherwise implied, presented to them, and understands that by knowingly and wilfully violating or refusing lawful orders is punishable at the discretion of the Supreme Court of Loquntia and court martial jurisdiction in accordance with the customs of the Royal Military and / or as prescribed by law, and in any inferior courts which may be ordained by Parliament.

    ORGANISATION.
    The party agrees to all of the terms and conditions of any order(s) of battle they have been enlisted into. They understand that their role in the Loquntian Military is not decided for themselves and they may not have a choice in the manner, location, leadership, and position in which they may be employed. I understand that I may from time to time be deployed onto campaigns or musters which in and of themselves consist of lawful orders which I must subordinate to.

    COMPENSATION.
    The undersigned party is eligible for pay in accordance with all applicable provisions according to Codes 100 and 10.IIX when actively reporting for duty in accordance with lawful orders, and in any other manner as prescribed by law. The undersigned party will not receive pay while in reserve or during training, or under any other circumstance according with military customs.

    DISMISSAL.
    Dismissal from the Loquntian Royal Military shall be granted:
       (A) Honourably or dishonourably at the discretion of the Loquntian Royal Military or as prescribed by law,
       (B) Upon request from the enlistee expressly within fourteen days of signing this contract, or
       (C) Honourably within fourteen days of sustaining a life-threatening injury arising from or associated with enlistment in the Loquntian Royal Military.
    Persons who are not deemed fit as personnel in the Royal Military may be dismissed from the Loquntian Royal Military immediately after enlistment.

    EQUIPMENT.
    The party may be given or be required to attain equipment or other assets on the behalf of the Loquntian Royal Military. Any such equipment is the property of the Royal Military and, except at the discretion thereof, must be returned on demand.

    VIOLATION OF TERMS OF ENLISTMENT CONTRACT.
    Violation of the terms and conditions of this contract shall be punishable at the discretion of the Loquntian Royal Military or as prescribed by law.

  12. Affirmation. Finally, you will be asked to type your full legal name and check the box to certify that you agree to all of the terms and conditions of the contract and to an anti-perjury agreement.
